Byte Size - HIXS's Technology Club

Welcome to the official website of Byte-Size, a club where we aim to educate students about the various 21st Century technologies around them so that they get a better understanding of how they work and how to work with them.

The club focuses on three areas of development: Website Development, Game Development and Artificial Intelligence to inculcate the skills of programming, logical and crtical thinking, commnuication and problem solving into our memebers, all while giving them a platform to achieve their creative and technological aspirations.
